**App tagline:** "Your complete Jiu-Jitsu training companion built for students, instructors, and gym administration."

**Terminology rule:** Never use "BJJ" anywhere in the app UI. Preferred replacements:
- "BJJ" → "Jiu-Jitsu" (formal/branded contexts)
- "BJJ" → "grappling" (casual/descriptive contexts)
- "BJJ student" → "grappler" or "student"
- "BJJ community" → "grappling community"

**Why:** User preference — Jiu-Jitsu and grappling are the preferred terms throughout the app.

**How to apply:** Any new UI copy, tour content, placeholder text, or labels should use Jiu-Jitsu or grappling instead of BJJ. Apply to all pages including tour, schedule, events, and gallery.
